Brand Overview

NeoLux Health delivers precision longevity medicine for executives in Dubai and London.

Client

Dr. Shabeena Aziz — Family Medicine Consultant & Longevity Physician with MSc in Sports & Exercise Medicine.

Locations

Dubai, UAE & London, UK — serving global executives who require flexibility.

Category

Precision Longevity Medicine — evidence-based healthspan optimization for high performers.

Audience

C-suite executives (40-65, $500K+) seeking measurable health outcomes, not wellness trends.

"Design a website that communicates scientific credibility and understated luxury—positioning Dr. Aziz as the trusted physician for executives who want evidence-based longevity protocols, not wellness trends."

05

Voice & Tone

Quietly authoritative. The visual equivalent of a Patek Philippe — refined craftsmanship that doesn't need to announce itself.

Authoritative

Speaks with quiet confidence, never oversells

Scientific

Uses precise language, avoids buzzwords

Refined

Understated luxury, never ostentatious

Personal

Dr. Aziz is front-and-center, not hidden behind a brand

Use This Language

  • "Precision longevity protocols"
  • "Evidence-based healthspan optimization"
  • "Measurable outcomes"
  • "Interventions" (not treatments)
  • "Protocols" (not programs)
  • "Book Consultation"

Avoid This Language

  • "Unlock your potential" (vague)
  • "Transform your life" (overused)
  • "Revolutionary treatments" (hyperbolic)
  • "Biohacking secrets" (trend-chasing)
  • "Holistic journey" (wellness-speak)
  • "Limited spots! Book now!" (urgency)

Imagery Guidelines

Warm, scientific, and sophisticated — never clinical or spa-like.

Warm marble textures
Abstract cellular forms
Professional portraiture

Imagery Do's

  • Abstract cellular/molecular forms
  • Light refracting through glass
  • Clean laboratory aesthetics
  • Architectural precision
  • Warm marble textures
  • Dr. Aziz in professional context
  • Subtle warm color grading

Imagery Don'ts

  • Generic stock photos of happy people
  • Obvious medical equipment
  • Spa/wellness imagery (candles, stones)
  • Busy, cluttered compositions
  • Cold, clinical environments
  • Overly posed or artificial portraits
  • Dubai bling aesthetic

Motion & Animation

Restrained but purposeful — every animation should feel measured, like a precise medical instrument.

Example: Subtle floating animation

Motion Principles

Earn Attention

Don't animate everything; make motion meaningful

Mathematical Precision

Use consistent timing, measured stagger delays

Subtle Depth

Parallax and layering create sophistication

Performance First

Smooth 60fps; prefer CSS transforms and opacity

Timing Guidelines

Element Duration Easing
Page transitions 0.6–0.8s cubic-bezier(0.16, 1, 0.3, 1)
Scroll reveals 0.6–1.0s ease-out
Hover states 0.3s ease
Micro-interactions 0.15–0.2s ease-out
Stagger delay 0.08–0.15s —

Motion Do's

  • Staggered text reveals
  • Scroll-triggered fade-ups
  • Subtle parallax depth
  • Animated stat counters
  • Smooth hover transitions

Motion Don'ts

  • Bouncy/elastic easing (too playful)
  • Excessive rotation or scaling
  • Animations that delay content access
  • Motion that distracts from content
  • Anything that feels "fun" rather than "refined"
